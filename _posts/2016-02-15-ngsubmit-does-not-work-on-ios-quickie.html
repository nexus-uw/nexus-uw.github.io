--- layout: post title: ngSubmit Does Not Work on iOS (Quickie Tip) date: '2016-02-15T09:00:00.002-08:00' author: Simon tags: - ngForm - iOS - ngSubmit - safari - angular modified_time: '2016-02-15T09:00:38.411-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-2267619345455483673.post-285082357919277850 blogger_orig_url: http://nexus-rage-quit.blogspot.com/2016/02/ngsubmit-does-not-work-on-ios-quickie.html --- If you have a <form> without a  button or a clickable input[type="submit'] inside of it on iOS Safari, ngSubmit has never worked for me.

I have been able to fix this issue by adding a input[type="submit'] with style="visibility: hidden;".
